Kanji Radicals Guide

Master characters by understanding their building blocks (部首 - Bushu). 🧩

💡 Tips for Learning Kanji

1
Kanji are Puzzles Understand that Kanji are not single pictures, but combinations of parts called "radicals".
2
Group by Radicals Divide Kanji into groups by their radicals and learn the common meanings they share.
3
Create Stories For example, in "休", a "person (人)" is next to a "tree (木)", so it means "rest". Turn radicals into parts of a story.
