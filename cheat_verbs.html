Verb Conjugation by Form

Master Japanese verbs visually by Hana Banana

Click on any card to hear the pronunciation!

1. Dictionary Form (基本形)

Casual present/future / Habitual action / Foundation for other rules

Ru-Verb (る動詞)
食べる taberu to eat
U-Verb (う動詞)
飲む nomu to drink
Irregular (する)
する suru to do
Irregular (くる)
来る kuru to come

2. Masu Form (ます形)

Polite present/future / Safe to use with strangers and teachers

-masu (Drop る)
食べます tabemasu eat / will eat
-imasu (u -> i)
飲みます nomimasu drink / will drink
Irregular (する)
します shimasu do / will do
Irregular (くる)
来ます kimasu come / will come

3. Te Form (て形)

Requests (-te kudasai) / Connecting actions (and then) / Present continuous (-te imasu)

-te (Drop る)
食べて tabete eat and...
-nde (mu -> nde)
飲んで nonde drink and...
Irregular (する)
して shite do and...
Irregular (くる)
来て kite come and...

4. Nai Form (ない形)

Casual negative / "Please don't" (-nai de kudasai) / "Must do" (-nakereba narimasen)

-nai (Drop る)
食べない tabenai do not eat
-anai (u -> a)
飲まない nomanai do not drink
Irregular (する)
しない shinai do not do
Irregular (くる)
来ない konai do not come

5. Ta Form (た形)

Casual Past tense / "Have experienced" (-ta koto ga arimasu)

-ta (Drop る)
食べた tabeta ate
-nda (mu -> nda)
飲んだ nonda drank
Irregular (する)
した shita did
Irregular (くる)
来た kita came

6. Potential Form (可能形)

"Can do" or "Are able to do" an action

-rareru (Drop る)
食べられる taberareru can eat
-eru (u -> e)
飲める nomeru can drink
Irregular (する)
できる dekiru can do
Irregular (くる)
来られる korareru can come

7. Provisional Conditional (~eba 形)

"If..." / Logical consequence or requirement

-reba (Drop る)
食べれば tabereba if (I) eat
-eba (u -> e)
飲めば nomeba if (I) drink
Irregular (する)
すれば sureba if (I) do
Irregular (くる)
来れば kureba if (I) come

8. Conditional (~tara 形)

"If..." / "When..." / Most versatile conditional form

-tara (From Ta-form)
食べたら tabetara if/when (I) eat
-dara (From Ta-form)
飲んだら nondara if/when (I) drink
Irregular (する)
したら shitara if/when (I) do
Irregular (くる)
来たら kitara if/when (I) come

9. Passive (受身形)

"To be [verb]-ed" / Used for actions happening to you

-rareru (Drop る)
食べられる taberareru to be eaten
-areru (u -> a)
飲まれる nomareru to be drunk
Irregular (する)
される sareru to be done
Irregular (くる)
来られる korareru to be come

10. Causative (使役形)

"To make/let someone do..."

-saseru (Drop る)
食べさせる tabesaseru make (someone) eat
-aseru (u -> a)
飲ませる nomaseru make (someone) drink
Irregular (する)
させる saseru make (someone) do
Irregular (くる)
来させる kosaseru make (someone) come
